Chef Edition

Graphic Design

As part of the i am talented programme (IAT) core team, I designed this booklet to reflect our collaborative work with various organizations, aiming to engage young minds in the culinary field.

Process

In the booklet's interior, I focused on creating an interactive environment for students to record their culinary journey and self-reflections. This included personalization spaces like areas for Polaroids and drawings, complemented by motivational quotes to inspire deeper thinking.

The layout was planned to guide students through their daily itineraries and recipes. Additionally, it introduced them to future educational opportunities in the culinary world, making these goals both inspiring and accessible, all while ensuring the learning experience remained fun and engaging.
